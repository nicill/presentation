# Presentation

My Name is Yago Diez and I am an Associate Professor at Yamagata University in Japan. I was born in Barcelona (Catalonia, Spain) and I have been living in Japan since 2015. I studied Math at university but I have always been interested in computers and how to use them to understand the world around us. That naturally led me to what today is called Data Science. 

- I enjoy researching applied projects usually using computer vision techniques. 
- I have been teaching Math, Programming and Data Science courses for almost 30 years now.
- When I am not working I love Sports, Boardgames, a strange mix of podcasts, TV shows and good, old fashioned books.
